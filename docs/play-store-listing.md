## Full description (1982 / 4000)

```
Cairn does one thing. It grows a streak for every day you do not open an app you chose to track. No lectures. No scolding. Just a calm record of the days you stayed away.

You pick the apps. Maybe a social app you reach for without thinking. Cairn watches only those, and counts each day you stay away.

WHAT IT DOES

- Grow a streak for every day you don't open a tracked app.
- See a current streak for each app you track.
- See an overall streak across all of your tracked apps.
- Read a short daily summary of how your streaks did.

HONEST BY DESIGN

Cairn does not round up. If it cannot confirm a day, it marks that day unverified and never counts it as a clean day. A streak only reflects days it could actually verify. Mark the days you stayed away, and trust what the number says.

A GENTLE PAUSE, IF YOU WANT IT

This part is optional and off until you turn it on. When you open a tracked app, Cairn can show a brief calm pause over it. A moment to decide. You can back out, or you can keep going. Cairn never blocks the app. The choice stays yours.

WHEN YOU ARE DONE WITH AN APP

If you uninstall a tracked app, Cairn marks it summited and keeps your record. You are free of it.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT

- Runs entirely on your device. No account, no sign-in, no servers, no cloud sync.
- The release build has no internet permission, so it cannot send your data anywhere.
- No ads. No tracking. No analytics.
- Works offline.

OPEN SOURCE

Cairn is free and open source under GPL-3.0. You can read every line.
Source code: https://github.com/Zansuken/cairn

A few lines from inside the app, so you know the tone:
- Grow a streak for every day you don't open a tracked app.
- Mark the days you stayed away.
- Unverified days are never counted clean.
- You're free.

Cairn is not therapy and not a cure. It just counts your days, honestly, and keeps that record on your phone. Mark the days. Watch them add up. When the app is gone, you're free.

Requires Android 8.0 and up.
```
